Brandon's Favorite Movies

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Revenant

Leo is top tier in this film, and it really showcases his acting range. In the movie, Leo is faced with a life altering incident, and againt unparalled odds he must fight for survival to exact revenge.

Man on Fire

Amazing movie about an ex CIA operative who has fallen from prestige due to his addiction to alcohol. He is hired by a wealthy couple as a private bodygaurd for their young daughter. Little does he know that this family is the target of a nefarious organization.

Avengers: End Game

Spectacular Sci-fi movie about superheros and villians.
